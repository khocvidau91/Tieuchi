# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We, the maintainers and contributors of Tieuchi, are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all people, regardless of their background or identity. We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### ✅ Examples of Behavior That Contributes to a Positive Environment

- **Being respectful** of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- **Giving and gracefully accepting** constructive criticism
- **Focusing on what is best** for the community
- **Showing empathy** towards other community members
- **Being patient and kind** in discussions and interactions
- **Welcoming newcomers** and helping them get started
- **Celebrating successes** and learning from failures together
- **Using inclusive language** and avoiding assumptions
- **Respecting privacy** and confidentiality of others

### ❌ Examples of Unacceptable Behavior

- The use of sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission (doxxing)
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Discrimination based on race, gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, caste, religion, nationality, or socioeconomic status
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Continued one-on-one communication after requests to cease
- Violent threats or incitement of violence

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces associated with the Tieuchi project, including:

- GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Project documentation and wiki
- Community forums and chat channels
- In-person or virtual events organized by the project
- Unofficial social media accounts associated with the project
- Any other spaces that represent the Tieuchi community

This Code of Conduct also applies when an individual is representing Tieuchi or its community in public spaces.

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Community members who witness or experience unacceptable behavior are encouraged to report it immediately. 

**How to Report**:

1. **GitHub Issues**: Open a confidential security advisory
   - Go to repository → Security tab → Report vulnerability
   - Title: "[CoC Violation] Brief description"
   - Keep details confidential

2. **Direct Email**: 
   - Email: conduct@xiaozhi.dev
   - Subject: "[Code of Conduct] Brief description"
   - Include relevant context and details

3. **Trusted Moderator**:
   - Contact a project maintainer directly
   - Provide details of the incident
   - Request confidentiality if needed

**What to Include in Your Report**:
- Description of the incident
- Involved parties
- When and where it occurred
- Any evidence (screenshots, links, etc.)
- Your contact information (optional)
- Desired resolution if applicable

### Investigation Process

1. **Receipt and Acknowledgment**
   - Report received within 24 hours
   - Investigator assigned
   - No public disclosure without consent

2. **Investigation**
   - Gather information from parties involved
   - Review evidence and context
   - Consult with community leaders if needed
   - Determine violations

3. **Resolution**
   - Communicate findings to reporter
   - Implement appropriate action
   - Follow-up to prevent recurrence
   - Appeal process available

### Consequences

Enforcement of the Code of Conduct will be fair and consistent. Possible consequences include:

| Level | Severity | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Minor | Warning, request for apology |
| 2 | Moderate | Temporary suspension from community |
| 3 | Severe | Extended suspension or ban |
| 4 | Critical | Permanent ban |

### Escalation Path

1. **First Violation**: Warning + guidance on CoC
2. **Second Violation**: Temporary suspension (1 week - 1 month)
3. **Third Violation**: Extended suspension (1 - 6 months)
4. **Severe/Repeated**: Permanent ban

Appeals can be made by contacting conduct@xiaozhi.dev within 30 days of the decision.

## Confidentiality

We take confidentiality seriously:

- Reporter identity protected if requested
- Details shared only with investigation team
- No public disclosure without consent
- Sensitive information handled securely
- Respecting privacy of all parties

## Community Responsibilities

### Maintainers

Maintainers have the responsibility to:
- Enforce this Code of Conduct
- Ensure fair and just treatment
- Maintain confidentiality of reports
- Lead by example
- Create a safe, welcoming environment

### Contributors

Contributors should:
- Follow the Code of Conduct
- Report violations promptly
- Respect others' perspectives
- Participate constructively
- Help maintain a positive community

### Community Members

Community members are encouraged to:
- Welcome new members
- Support others
- Report problematic behavior
- Help resolve conflicts peacefully
- Model good behavior

## Expected Behavior

### In Code Reviews

```
✅ GOOD:
"I think there might be a simpler approach here. 
Consider using X instead of Y for better performance. 
What do you think?"

❌ BAD:
"This code is terrible. You should know better."
```

### In Discussions

```
✅ GOOD:
"I see your point. However, I have concerns about X. 
Let's discuss the tradeoffs..."

❌ BAD:
"You're wrong and clearly don't understand the problem."
```

### In Bug Reports

```
✅ GOOD:
"I found that feature X doesn't work as expected in scenario Y. 
Here are steps to reproduce..."

❌ BAD:
"This app is broken. You devs don't know what you're doing!"
```

## FAQ

### Q: What if I'm not sure if something violates the CoC?

A: When in doubt, reach out to the maintainers. We're here to help clarify and guide.

### Q: Will my report be kept confidential?

A: Yes, unless you specify otherwise. We only share details with those necessary for investigation.

### Q: Can I appeal a decision?

A: Yes, you have 30 days to appeal any enforcement action. Email conduct@xiaozhi.dev.

### Q: What if the reporter is the one violating the CoC?

A: All reports are investigated fairly. If the reporter violated CoC, appropriate action is taken.

### Q: How long are enforcement actions?

A: Depends on severity:
- Warning: Ongoing until behavior changes
- Suspension: 1 week to 6 months
- Ban: Permanent

### Q: Can I return after a ban?

A: Permanent bans are final. Appeals can be made after 12 months.
